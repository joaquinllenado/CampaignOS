CampaignOS — AI-Native Influencer Campaign Intelligence Agent
Overview
We are building CampaignOS: an AI-native campaign intelligence and optimization agent designed to evaluate influencer campaigns across the full funnel based on the client’s business objective.
Instead of focusing only on reporting vanity metrics, the platform autonomously:
understands campaign goals


defines success criteria


evaluates creator performance


explains why campaigns succeeded or failed


recommends optimization actions


The goal is to help brands prove that influencer campaigns are driving real business outcomes across:
Awareness


Engagement


Sales / TikTok Shop conversion


This moves beyond dashboards into autonomous growth intelligence and campaign strategy.


Core Problem
Most influencer marketing platforms:
focus on surface-level metrics


stop at reporting

cannot connect creator behavior to business impact


Brands still struggle to answer:
Did the campaign actually work?


Which creators drove meaningful outcomes?

What should be optimized next?

Agentic Workflow
Step 1 — Campaign Intake
The brand uploads:
campaign brief


objectives


audience


budget


product/category


Nia indexes:
market research


creator briefs


campaign history


PDFs and supporting documentation



Step 2 — Dynamic Success Framework
The AI agent autonomously builds:
KPI hierarchy


funnel weighting


success criteria


campaign evaluation logic


Example
Awareness Campaign:
50% reach


20% completion rate


20% sentiment


10% shares


Sales Campaign:
40% GMV


30% conversion rate


20% CTR


10% CPA efficiency


The framework adapts based on business intent rather than using static reporting.

Step 3 — Creator Discovery & Ranking
Using Reacher’s Social Intelligence data, the agent:
identifies creators


evaluates audience fit


analyzes historical performance


predicts funnel alignment


ranks creators based on campaign objectives


The focus is not “best creators overall,” but:
“best creators for this specific business objective.”

Step 4 — Autonomous Campaign Build
The agent:
creates creator shortlists


drafts outreach


structures campaign cohorts


recommends budget allocation


explains campaign rationale


All actions are executed through sandboxed endpoints.

Step 5 — Continuous Campaign Monitoring
The agent continuously monitors:
creator spikes


trend shifts


engagement quality


GMV anomalies


underperforming segments



Step 6 — Attribution Forensics
Instead of only reporting results, the agent explains:
why performance changed


which creators drove impact


which content formats influenced outcomes


where the funnel broke


which audience segments responded


Example
“The campaign underperformed in conversion not due to low reach, but because entertainment-heavy content generated attention without product comprehension.”

Step 7 — Autonomous Optimization
The agent recommends:
increasing or reducing creator spend


shifting creative direction


modifying CTA strategy


reallocating budget


changing creator archetypes


testing new audience segments


This creates a full AI-native optimization loop.
