Awareness Intelligence Framework (V3 — High Signal / Low Noise)
Objective
The Awareness framework evaluates:
how effectively a campaign generated visibility


how audiences responded to the content


how efficiently creators amplified awareness


whether the campaign expanded beyond existing audiences


whether awareness momentum was sustained over time


The framework is designed to:
avoid vanity metrics


avoid overfitting to viral spikes


prioritize explainability


provide strategic interpretation instead of analytics overload


This framework should feel like:
campaign intelligence,
not:
reporting.

Core Principles
1. Awareness ≠ Sales
Awareness campaigns are evaluated on:
visibility


audience resonance


creator amplification


momentum


NOT:
GMV


purchases


conversion efficiency



2. Benchmarking Is Contextual
Campaigns should be compared against:
similar awareness campaigns


similar creator tiers


similar campaign structures


the brand’s own historical performance


NOT against:
a universal benchmark



3. The AI Interprets Metrics
The system should explain:
what happened


why it matters


what to do next


The user should NOT need to interpret:
percentiles


benchmark calculations


weighted scoring logic


Those remain internal.

Benchmark Layers
The framework compares campaigns against multiple benchmark contexts.

Platform Benchmark
“How did this perform compared to overall awareness campaigns?”

Tier Benchmark
“How did this perform compared to campaigns with similar creator scale and campaign structure?”

Brand Benchmark
“How did this perform compared to the brand’s historical campaigns?”

Cohort Benchmark (Future Layer)
“How did this perform compared to campaigns using similar creator and content strategies?”

Campaign Data vs Benchmark Data
Always separate:
What happened
from:
What it means relative to benchmarks
Example:
Campaign Performance
“The campaign maintained steady visibility across multiple creators.”
Benchmark Context
“Visibility remained healthy relative to similar awareness campaigns, but creator expansion was weaker than expected.”

Sample Output Template
Awareness — Healthy
So What?
This campaign successfully maintained audience attention and generated healthy engagement, but it did not significantly expand beyond the brand’s existing creator network.
Performance was stronger in audience interaction than in pure reach efficiency, meaning people who saw the content engaged with it, but overall visibility growth remained moderate relative to similar awareness campaigns.

What Happened
The campaign sustained steady visibility across multiple creators and built momentum later in the campaign window.
Audience interaction remained healthy throughout the campaign, especially on review-style and POV content formats.
The strongest awareness moments came from a small number of high-performing posts rather than broad creator amplification.

Compared To Similar Campaigns
Compared to similar awareness campaigns:
engagement quality was stronger than normal


visibility was healthy but not exceptional


creator expansion was weaker than expected


momentum improved late in the campaign rather than at launch


Compared to the brand’s historical campaigns:
audience interaction improved


creator-network growth remained flat



What Worked Best
Review and POV-style content generated the strongest audience interaction.


Consistent posting helped maintain awareness momentum across the week.


Mid-campaign visibility acceleration helped sustain attention over time.



What Limited Performance
The campaign relied heavily on existing creators rather than expanding into new creator audiences.


Visibility was distributed across many posts, but few individual videos significantly broke out.


Early campaign momentum was weaker than later campaign performance.



What To Change Right Now
Prioritize onboarding new creators earlier in the campaign lifecycle.


Double down on review and POV-style content formats.


Improve launch coordination to create stronger first-week awareness spikes.


Focus on fewer, stronger-performing content concepts instead of relying on posting volume alone.
